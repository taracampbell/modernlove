Modern Love

Can a mobile dating app be better designed to match Canadian users’ expectations? Participate in our study!

Our Study

Are you over the age of 18 and have you used or are currently using mobile dating apps? Have you thought about using mobile dating apps but never signed up? If so, we need you!

To get a sense of users’ relationships to and experiences with the mobile dating apps that they’ve used, we are piloting a unique design research method: we’re asking prospective participants to write a love letter or break-up letter to a dating app of your choice. The activity should take no more than 20 minutes to complete. Your letter can be submitted electronically and anonymously through this form. The data we gather will be used to understand what users value and expect from mobile dating apps, and identify possible pain points and opportunities for better design.

Letters

We're employing a unique design research method: write us a love letter or breakup letter to a mobile dating app!

These are the steps:

1 Brainstorm (2 minutes) Brainstorm a list of mobile dating apps that you’ve used or have considered using.

2 Explore your experience (2 minutes) Think of the experiences you’ve had with each one. Pick one that you feel has impacted you the most - it can be associated with a positive experience, a negative experience, or something in between.

3 Write a letter (10 minutes) Now imagine the app is a person. Imagine it’s your significant other.

Love Letter

If you’ve had a positive experience with it, we’d like for you to write it a love letter. Feel free to use the following prompts as a springboard for your thoughts:

  • How did you meet?
  • What made you fall for it?
  • When did you know?
  • Why do you choose to stay with it, even as others compete for your attention?
  • What are the most memorable moments you’ve shared?
  • How does it bring out the best in you?
  • How does it make your day better?
Break Up Letter

If you’ve had a negative experience with it, we’d like for you to write it a break up letter. Feel free to use the following prompts as a springboard for your thoughts:

  • How did you meet?
  • Why did you give it a chance?
  • When did you know it wasn’t going to work out? What were the signs?
  • How did/does it make you feel?
  • Why are you better off without it?
  • What would it need to do if it wanted you back?
  • What do others have that it doesn’t?
  • Is there anything you’ll miss about it?

4 Record yourself (optional) (10 minutes) If you feel comfortable doing, we encourage you to record and submit a video of you narrating your love letter/break-up letter. Please note that this component is optional.
